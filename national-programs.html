Philippine Plan for Gender Responsive Development (1995-2025)

This program outlines a 30-year strategic framework for integrating gender equality and empowerment into all government policies, programs, and projects.

Gender and Development Budget (GAD)

The GAD budget is an allocation in the national and local government budgets aimed at addressing gender issues and advancing gender equality.

Framework Plan for Women

This plan provides a policy structure for implementing gender-sensitive development programs and creating opportunities for women in various sectors.
