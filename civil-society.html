Women's Involvement in Civil Society

Click on a law to view its details:

The 1987 Constitution (Article 13 Section 14)

This law mandates the recognition and promotion of the rights of women, ensuring gender equality and participation in all aspects of society.

Local Government Code of 1991

This code provides for the integration of gender-responsive measures in local government units, ensuring women's participation in governance.

Party List Law

The Party List Law ensures women's representation in Congress by allocating party-list seats for marginalized and underrepresented groups, including women.

Women in Nation Building Law (RA 7192)

RA 7192 recognizes women's active role in nation-building and provides equal opportunities in various sectors, including education and employment.

Labor Code (1989)

The Labor Code includes provisions to ensure gender equality in the workplace, prohibit discrimination, and provide maternity benefits.

1988 Comprehensive Agrarian Reform Law

This law provides for the equal right of women to own land and participate in agrarian reform programs.
