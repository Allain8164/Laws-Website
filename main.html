Call to Action

Steps for Raising Awareness:
- Conduct barangay-level workshops on gender-based violence.
- Partner with local schools to introduce education on gender equality and women’s rights.
- Collaborate with media outlets for public service announcements.
- Engage civil society groups like Gabriela and PILIPINA for grassroots advocacy campaigns.
- Establish support groups for survivors to share experiences and access assistance.
